About the Project

The Automated Multi-Vegetable Transplanter is an innovative agricultural machine designed to automate the process of planting vegetable seedlings into raised bed fields. Traditional transplanting is labor-intensive, time-consuming, and often inconsistent, leading to reduced crop survival rates. This machine eliminates manual intervention by integrating automated systems for sapling pickup, conveying, soil digging, planting, and soil covering.

With adjustable ground clearance, precise spacing, and gentle handling of delicate seedlings, the system ensures uniform planting depth and strong root establishment. By reducing human effort and improving efficiency, the transplanter not only enhances productivity but also contributes to sustainable farming practices.
