About the Project

The Onion Harvester is an innovative agricultural machine designed to automate the process of harvesting onions from fields. Traditional harvesting is labor-intensive, time-consuming, and often causes damage to the crop bulbs. This machine eliminates manual intervention by integrating automated systems for bulb extraction, soil separation, and collection.

With smart depth sensors, precise extraction mechanisms, and gentle handling of delicate bulbs, the system ensures minimal crop damage and efficient harvesting. By reducing human effort and improving efficiency, the harvester not only enhances productivity but also contributes to sustainable farming practices.
